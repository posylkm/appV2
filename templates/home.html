Who can register as a developer?

Developers with a verifiable track record in residential, commercial, or mixed-use projects.

Who can register as an investor?

Institutional and professional investors seeking secured, short-term real estate exposure.

Who can view projects?

Registered investors can browse full details and documents. Public visitors can only view high-level information on the homepage.

Is the platform regulated as a financial intermediary?

No. RE Marketplace operates as an information and introduction platform. We do not provide investment advice, arrange or broker deals, hold client money, or distribute securities. All engagement and transactions occur directly between developers and investors following bilateral agreement.

What’s the typical project duration?

Most opportunities are between 3 and 24 months.

What qualifies as a secured project?

Projects with collateral such as property, land, or first/second-charge security.

Can investors contact developers directly?

Initial contact occurs through the platform; deeper info requires NDA.

What is the tri-party NDA process?

When an investor expresses interest in a project, they may request to sign a tri-party non-disclosure agreement between the Developer, the Platform, and the Investor. Once signed, the developer can share confidential information such as financial model, Quantity Surveyor (QS) reports, title documents and planning details securely.

Does the NDA grant investment rights?

No — the NDA only governs confidentiality and information access. Any subsequent investment decision, term sheet, or legal agreement is negotiated directly between the developer and investor.

What fees are charged?

Registration is free for both developers and investors. RE Marketplace may charge a service or facilitation fee for certain introductions or data access — these are agreed transparently and in writing before any engagement.

How is project information verified?

Developers are responsible for the accuracy of their submissions. RE Marketplace performs basic plausibility checks but does not conduct full due diligence or guarantee completeness.

Data & privacy

We use role-based access and all information is stored securely. Only owners (or admins) can edit or modify projects.

How is data stored and protected?

All uploaded files are stored securely with access restricted by user role. Developers retain ownership of their materials. We comply with applicable data protection laws and do not sell or share user data with third parties.

Can projects be edited or removed?

Yes. Developers can edit or withdraw their own projects at any time. Admins may remove listings that breach content or eligibility standards.

How do investors search for projects?

Investors can filter projects by location, risk level, IRR, security type, or duration. Summaries are visible to all registered investors, with additional details released once an NDA is in place.

Can multiple investors participate in a single project?

Potentially, yes — where a developer is open to co-investment or syndication. Such arrangements are managed outside the platform and coordinated directly between the parties.
